Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Velion?

Velion is an all-in-one Discord bot offering features like moderation, utility, fun, and user management commands. It is designed to enhance your server experience.

How do I upgrade to a premium plan?

You can upgrade to a premium plan by selecting one of the options in our store and completing the payment via PayPal.

What are the benefits of upgrading to Velion Premium?

Upgrading to Velion Premium unlocks exclusive features such as enhanced moderation tools, advanced AFK system, custom embeds, priority support, and more. Premium users also receive regular updates and early access to new features.

How can I Activate Velion Premium on my Discord server?

Once you've purchased a premium plan, use the `/use_key ` command on your server to activate premium features. You will receive a unique key after payment, which can be used to activate Velion Premium.

Can I transfer my Velion Premium subscription to another Discord server?

Yes, Velion Premium subscriptions are transferable to another server. Use the `/transfer ` command to transfer your subscription. This feature is available for the lifetime and yearly plans.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel your subscription, premium features will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle. Afterward, your server will revert to the free version, but you will not lose any data or settings.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for the lifetime plan. If you are not satisfied within 7 days of purchase, contact our support for a full refund. For monthly and yearly plans, refunds are only available within the first 48 hours of activation.

Will my premium subscription auto-renew?

No, the monthly and yearly premium subscriptions will not auto-renew.

How do I get support as a premium user?

Premium users receive priority support. You can open a ticket in our support Discord server or email us directly for assistance. We aim to respond to premium users within 24 hours.

What happens if my server exceeds the usage limits for free users?

Free users have access to a limited set of features. If your server exceeds the usage limits (such as command usage or message counts), you may experience reduced functionality. Premium users do not have these limitations and get unlimited access to all features.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime. Monthly subscribers can cancel without additional fees, but refunds are not available for used time.
